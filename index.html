How We Use Your Data

Adidas uses data collected from wearable devices to provide a better and more personalized experience. Here's how we use your data:

  • Personalized Product Recommendations:

    Based on your activity levels and fitness goals, we recommend products that best suit your needs. For example, if your wearable device indicates that you are a runner, we might suggest our latest running shoes and related accessories.

  • Customized Workout Plans:

    Using your fitness data, we create workout plans tailored to your performance and goals. If your device shows that you need to improve your cardiovascular health, we provide specific exercise routines to help you achieve this.

  • Enhanced Customer Support:

    By understanding your usage patterns, our customer support team can offer more relevant advice and solutions. If your device shows you are facing issues with a particular type of exercise, our support can provide tips and product suggestions to help.

Your Data, Your Control

At Adidas, we prioritize your privacy and data security. Here's how we protect and use your data:

  • Data Encryption: All collected data is encrypted to ensure it remains secure.
  • User Control: You have full control over your data. You can view, edit, or delete your data anytime through our app settings.
  • Transparent Policies: We provide clear and transparent information about how your data is used.
